ARC Membership & Logistics

Becoming a member of the Art Rotation Co. is the first step toward building a flexible, high-end art collection.

01. Join the Collective

Register for free to receive updates on new works, exclusive "first look" gallery previews, and access to the rotation program.

02. Select & Rotate

Once you choose a piece, we arrange the White-Glove delivery. Your 4% monthly fee begins only once the piece is installed in your space.

03. Equity & Statements

As a member, 75% of your fees are tracked as **Art Equity**. Every quarter, you receive a personal statement showing your balance, which can be applied to the outright purchase of any piece in the collection.
