White-Glove Terms

1. The Rotation Model
Art Rotation Co. (ARC) provides a monthly subscription service. You pay 4% of the artwork's value to enjoy the piece in your home or office.

2. Equity Accumulation
75% of your monthly fee is applied as a credit toward the eventual purchase of the piece. If you choose to keep the work permanently, your total payments act as a down payment.

3. White-Glove Swaps
When you are ready for a new piece, a flat **$75 - $125 Rotation Fee** covers the professional logistics: insured pickup of the current work and delivery/installation of the new selection.

4. Care of Work
Collectors are responsible for maintaining the work in its original condition while on rotation. All pieces are insured during transport.
